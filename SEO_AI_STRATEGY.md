# SEO_AI_STRATEGY.md

## Purpose
This guide defines how to structure Procitec.io for SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and AI discoverability.

Goal:
- Attract qualified industrial buyers
- Improve visibility in Google and AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity)
- Increase engagement and conversions

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Intent-driven content
Focus on specific industrial problems, not generic marketing.

Bad:
"We provide industrial solutions"

Good:
"Preventive Maintenance Software for Manufacturing Plants"

---

### 2. One page = one problem
Each page must target a single use case or problem.

Examples:
- /eam-software-for-manufacturing
- /energy-management-system-industrial
- /preventive-maintenance-system
- /asset-management-software-india

---

## Page Structure (MANDATORY)

### Title
Clear, keyword-based
Example:
Preventive Maintenance Software for Manufacturing | Procitec

### Sections
- Introduction (problem + audience)
- Problem explanation
- Solution (Procitec product)
- Features / capabilities
- Use cases
- Industries
- Benefits
- CTA

---

## FAQ Section (CRITICAL)

Every page must include FAQ.

Examples:
- What is EAM software?
- Who should use EMS?
- How does preventive maintenance work?

Purpose:
- Improves SEO
- Helps AI extract answers

---

## AI Optimization Rules

### Write for AI systems:
- Use clear sentences
- Use definitions
- Use bullet points
- Avoid vague language

Example:

Good:
"Procitec EAM is a software system used to manage assets and maintenance in industrial plants."

Bad:
"Our platform revolutionizes digital transformation."

---

## Content Layers

### Layer 1: Product Pages
- EAM
- EMS

### Layer 2: Problem Pages
- Preventive maintenance
- Asset tracking
- Energy monitoring

### Layer 3: Industry Pages
- Manufacturing
- Utilities
- Infrastructure

---

## Internal Linking

- Link homepage → product pages
- Link product pages → problem pages
- Link problem pages → industry pages

Purpose:
- Improves SEO authority
- Helps navigation

---

## Technical SEO

### Required:
- Title tags
- Meta descriptions
- Proper headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Fast loading pages
- Optimized images

---

## Performance Rules

- Keep pages lightweight
- Avoid heavy scripts
- Optimize images

Fast sites rank better and retain users.

---

## Codex Implementation Rules

When generating pages:

- Follow page structure
- Add SEO metadata
- Include FAQ
- Use proper headings
- Maintain internal linking

Avoid:
- keyword stuffing
- generic content
- vague messaging

---

## Priority Execution

### Phase 1
- Homepage SEO basics
- EAM product page
- EMS placeholder page

### Phase 2
- 3–5 problem-based pages
- FAQ sections
- internal linking

### Phase 3
- blog content
- case studies
- deeper industry pages

---

## Final Principle

SEO + AI success =

Clear content + structured information + real-world relevance

If both humans and machines understand your content easily,
your site will rank and convert.
