# Procitec.io Project Brief

## Purpose
Procitec.io is the product platform website for Procitec applications.

It is NOT the main corporate website.
The corporate website remains procitec.in.

## Goal
Present Procitec applications clearly and professionally, and direct users to:
- application product pages
- login/app entry points
- support or demo requests

## Applications
- EAM: https://eam.procitec.io/
- EMS: https://ems.procitec.io/ (planned)

## Audience
- Industrial buyers
- Plant heads
- Maintenance managers
- Energy managers
- Technical decision makers
