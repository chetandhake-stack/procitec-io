# ENGAGEMENT_STRATEGY.md

## Purpose
This guide defines how to design Procitec.io to maximize user engagement and conversion.

This is NOT about visual beauty.
This is about clarity, trust, and guiding users toward action.

---

### 1. Hero Section

- What is this?
- Who is it for?
- What should I do next?
