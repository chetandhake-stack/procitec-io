# Content Guide

## Writing Style
- Clear
- Direct
- Trustworthy
- Engineering-led
- Business-friendly

## Avoid
- Buzzwords
- Over-promising
- Vague language
- Startup hype tone

## Emphasize
- Reliability
- Efficiency
- Visibility
- Asset intelligence
- Energy intelligence
- Industrial operations

## Tone Examples

### Good
"Improve asset reliability and reduce downtime."

### Bad
"Revolutionize your digital transformation journey."

## CTA Style
- Book Demo
- Talk to Expert
- Explore Application
